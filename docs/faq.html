However, can you do us a favor? Let us know about your planned visit by scheduling time on our farm calendar. This helps us keep the farm comfortable and fun for all.

Our farm's mission is to foster free community recreational opportunities for people with disabilities who often experience barriers in other settings. Because of this, we prioritize our farm amenities for folks with disabilities, their family members and caregivers. We love welcoming all forms of visitors. However, if there are space or time constraints, we will prioritize those with the greatest need. We appreciate your understanding in centering our mission!

The farm does not provide caregivers, Personal Care Assistants (PCAs) or other specific support on-site. We work to create a proactively accessible and inclusive environment to reduce potential barriers. However, if you have questions or accessibility ideas that would make a farm visit more viable for you, contact us and we'll think through ideas together.

We ask that you leave your pets at home. However, service animals are always welcome.

We review our facilities often for opportunities to enhance physical-, medical- and sensory-related accessibility. We are always making improvements and welcome your feedback should you recognize additional needs. At this time, we have:

  • Indoor
    • Wheelchair-friendly recreation center with smooth floors.
    • Two wheelchair accessible restrooms: one includes a hoyer lift and adult changing table.
    • Air-conditioning/heating.
    • Accessible driveway drop-off.
    • A serving kitchen with microwave & fridge that can be locked.
  • Outdoor
    • Covered pavilion.
    • Paved walking/rolling/biking trail.
    • Completely fenced-in outdoor recreation area.
    • Wheelchair accessible pergola swing.
    • Ramped pontoon boat.

Due to the farm's drop-in nature, we have scaled down our animal operations. However, the farm is working to build specific programs to interact with guest animals alongside staff in the future. If you're looking for a traditional farm animal experience, we encourage you to head five miles down the road to Windy Willow Farm Adventure.

The farm periodically hosts short-term programs and projects, which you can see on our Programs & Events page. At this time, we are working to expand our program offerings so that there are more structured opportunities to interact with the farm.

We are incorporated as “A Great Day Farm,” have no connection to Great Day Farms and do not sell eggs.
