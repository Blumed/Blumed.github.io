Visiting the Farm

A Great Day Farm offers free indoor and outdoor space to individuals, families and small groups on a pre-registration basis. We foster a proactively accessible & inclusive environment that removes barriers so that visitors can show up (joy)fully.

Pre Register

Your Visit We ask that you pre-register your visit to the farm. This improves the experience for everybody. It ensures that the space is not crowded and remains comfortable and accessible for all.

Staffing

We do not provide on-site staff during visiting hours, including caregivers or Personal Care Assistants (PCAs). If you have specific needs, please bring support with you.
