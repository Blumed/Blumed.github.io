Visit the Farm

A Great Day Farm offers free indoor and outdoor space to individuals with disabilities, their families and small groups. We foster a proactively accessible & inclusive environment that removes barriers so that visitors can show up (joy)fully.

Amenities

We offer indoor and outdoor options for guests to try with a goal of supporting a diversity of ages, access needs and levels of adventure.

Indoor Recreation Center

A 3500 square foot pole barn with restrooms, a kitchen and various play zones/activities. You can check out:

  • Indoor Sports (Basketball, Air Hockey, Foosball, Table Tennis)
  • Indoor Swings
  • Musical instruments
  • Latch boards/fidgets
  • Enclosed Patio/Seating Area
  • Rocking Chairs

Outdoor Recreation

3 acres of fenced-in outdoor space with:

  • Walking/rolling/biking paths totaling ⅓ of a mile
  • Adaptive bicycles
  • Sensory gardens
  • Interactive play spaces (a grain mill, a pontoon boat, sand pit, and more)
  • Swings
  • Covered pavilion and picnic tables
  • Adirondack chairs and park benches
  • Bocce ball court

Restrooms

Our facilities include two wheelchair accessible private room restrooms. These include:

  • Grab bars
  • Roll-under sinks
  • Supplies at wheelchair height
  • One restroom includes an adult changing table and hoyer lift

Kitchen

The recreation center has a kitchen with key access that includes a fridge, microwave, sink, and coffee maker as well as a serving window that can be opened up.

Staffing

We do not provide on-site staff during visiting hours, including caregivers or Personal Care Assistants (PCAs). If you have specific needs, please bring support with you.
