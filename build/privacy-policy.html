Last Updated: Augest 2024

Introduction

A Great Day Farm Foundation ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, www.agreatdayfarm.org (the "Site") and use our services.

Information We Collect

1. Information We Collect 1.1 Personal Information We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless you voluntarily provide it to us. This includes information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to share when you sign up for our email lists, make a donation, or contact us through our Site. 1.2 Non-Personal Information We collect non-personal information automatically when you visit our Site. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages you visit, and the time and date of your visits. This information is used to improve our Site and services and to ensure that they are working correctly. 2. Use of Google Analytics We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze information about how visitors use our Site. Google Analytics uses cookies to track your interactions with our Site. The information collected by these cookies helps us understand user behavior and improve our Site and services. For more information on Google Analytics' data practices, please visit Google Analytics Privacy Policy. 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies 3.1 Cookies A cookie is a small file placed on your device that helps us understand your preferences and improve your experience on our Site. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our Site may not function properly. 3.2 Other Tracking Technologies In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies such as web beacons or pixels to collect information about your use of our Site. 4. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: Improve our Site and services. Understand how visitors interact with our Site. Communicate with you regarding your inquiries or donations. Send you newsletters or updates if you have opted in. 5. Sharing Your Information We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except as required by law or with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our Site, conducting our business, or servicing you, provided that those parties agree to keep this information confidential. 6. Data Security We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. 7. Your Choices You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions included in those communications. 8. Links to Other Sites Our Site may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit. 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. 10. Contact Us
